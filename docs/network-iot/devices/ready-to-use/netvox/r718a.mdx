## R718A Wireless Temperature and Humidity Sensor for Low-Temperature Environment

The R718A is capable to measure temperature and humidity in a low-temperature environment. The R718A
can be used in domestic and commercial refrigerators to store and transport food, medicines, flowers
and other perishable goods, on walls or in logistics refrigerators. Suitable as a data logger.
