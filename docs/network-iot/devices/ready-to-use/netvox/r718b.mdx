## R718B Wireless Resistance Temperature Detector

The R718B connects an external resistance temperature detector (PT1000) to measure the temperature.

### Specifications

#### LoRaWAN device type:

- Class A end-device

#### Sensors:

- temperature
- battery

#### Dimensions:

\*\* Main device:

- width: 88.19 mm
- length: 112 mm
- height: 32 mm

\*\* L-type probe:

- diameter: 5 mm
- length: 100 + 60 mm

\*\* Needle probe:

- diameter: 5 mm
- length: 150 mm

\*\* Round head probe (PT1000):

- diameter: 5 mm
- length: 30 mm

#### Weight:

- 141 grams

#### Battery:

- type: 2x ER14505 Lithium AA battery / 3.6 V / 2400 mAh per battery
- replaceable: True

### Note:

- The base is attached to a magnet that can be attached to a ferromagnetic material object.
- Alerts can be set via SMS text and email (optional).
- This detector can be chosen with 3 types of probes: round head probe (PT1000), needle probe and
  L-type probe.
- All probes are made of 316 stainless steel.
- Lead length: 2 m
